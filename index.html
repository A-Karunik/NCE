Welcome to NCE

National College of Engineering (NCE) is a premier institution dedicated to delivering excellence in technical education, innovation, and research. Established with the vision of shaping skilled engineers and future leaders, NCE has become one of the most trusted engineering colleges offering a transformative learning experience.


At NCE, we believe engineering is more than academics — it is about creating solutions, building technology, and driving progress. Our campus offers modern classrooms, advanced research labs, and an environment that encourages creativity and exploration.


Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality engineering education with innovation and research.
  • Nurture technically skilled, ethical, and socially responsible professionals.
  • Promote creativity, problem-solving, and continuous learning.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized engineering institution empowering students to drive technological advancements and contribute positively to society.
